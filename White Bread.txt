400ml of water 110f
    Take 100ml of this and mix with packet of instant yeast (1/4oz) and 325g AP flour, 50g sugar, 5g salt, 45g unsalted butter at room temp
Stir then add remaining 300ml water then keep stiring and add remaining flour then knead out for 7-9min until smooth.
Oil a bowl and let rise until doubled (~1hr)
Punch down then form into loaf by tucking it under itself.
Put in bread pan lined with parchment paper and butter.
Cover with plastic.
Let double in size again (~1hr)
Brush top with butter
Bake at 400 for 25-35min.
Internal temp needs to be 200f
Brush with butter
Let cool completely
